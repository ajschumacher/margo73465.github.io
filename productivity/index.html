Productivity Analysis

This is a visualization of personal data that I collected about my productivity at different times of the day. For 8 weeks I kept a log of what I did for each 30 minute increment of my workday in a Google Docs Spreadsheet. I was then able to use a Google Apps Script (similar to Javascript) to analyze the text, assign a productivity score to each box, and ultimately calculate my average productivity for each day and time period. I used D3 to visualize this data and create the graphic below. The line to the right shows the marginal distribution of my productivity by time of day.
